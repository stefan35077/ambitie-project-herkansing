# MoSCoW – Stellar Spheres

## Must Have
- Shooting mechanism with mouse-only controls
- Random orb generation after each shot
- Orb matching (3 or more of the same color)
- Orb pathways that move toward an endpoint
- Win condition when all orbs are cleared
- Lose condition when an orb reaches the endpoint
- Multiple playable levels

## Should Have
- Power-ups (Freeze, Rainbow, Black Hole)
- Chain reactions when matches connect
- Three-star level rating system
- Clear visual feedback for matches and power-ups

## Could Have
- Black hole obstacles that affect shot direction
- Different pathway shapes (orbits, constellations)
- Increased difficulty in later levels
- Sound effects

## Won’t Have
- Multiplayer
- Story or narrative elements
- Cosmetic customization
- Mobile touch controls
